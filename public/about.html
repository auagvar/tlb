For Armenia and Artsakh


Hundreds of kids from Artsakh need your help. After the war, many families have been displayed and with that many children got behind from their classes and normal life. TLB is an initiative to gather all the volunteers’ information who want to teach a class online or offline on one platform. TLB allows filtering the classes and timeslots more conveniently for the user. Currently, each contribution is vital for our future victory. Overall, education is the most powerful weapon which can be used to change the world. Sign up as a teacher now!
